## 🧾 **Step-by-Step Guide: Excel-Based Stock Portfolio Tracker**

---

### 🔹 Step 1: **Prepare Your Portfolio Data**

Create a table like this:

| Ticker | Company Name | Quantity | Buy Price | Current Price | Total Value | Gain/Loss |
|--------|--------------|----------|-----------|----------------|-------------|-----------|
| AAPL   | Apple Inc.   | 10       | 150       | [Live]         | [=Qty*Price] | [=Gain]   |
| MSFT   | Microsoft    | 5        | 280       | [Live]         | …           | …         |

---

### 🔹 Step 2: **Fetch Real-Time Prices (Excel 365)**

#### ✅ Using Excel 365:
1. Enter Ticker symbols (e.g., AAPL, MSFT)
2. Select them → Go to **Data tab → Stocks** → Converts ticker to stock-linked data
3. Insert "Price", "Name", "Market Cap", etc. via **Insert Data → Field Selector** or use formulas like:

```excel
=[@Ticker].Price
```

---

### 🔹 Step 3: **Auto Calculate Portfolio Metrics**

#### ✅ Formulas:
- **Total Value**: `=[@Quantity]*[@Current Price]`
- **Gain/Loss**: `=[@Total Value] - ([@Quantity]*[@Buy Price])`
- **Gain %**: `=[@Gain/Loss]/([@Quantity]*[@Buy Price])`

---

### 🔹 Step 4: **Portfolio Summary**

Use these metrics:
- Total Investment: `=SUM(Quantity * Buy Price)`
- Current Value: `=SUM(Total Value)`
- Net Gain/Loss: `=SUM(Gain/Loss)`
- Average Return: `=AVERAGE(Gain%)`

---

### 🔹 Step 5: **Add Charts & Visualizations**

Create:
- Pie Chart: % allocation by stock
- Bar Chart: Gain/Loss by stock
- Trend line: Price over time (use Stock History if available)

---

## 🌐 **Alternative: Google Sheets Version (Free)**

In Google Sheets, use:

```excel
=GOOGLEFINANCE("AAPL","price")
=GOOGLEFINANCE("AAPL", "price", DATE(2023,1,1), TODAY(), "DAILY")
```

🔄 Auto-updates every 2 minutes (though delayed quotes).
